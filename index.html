Shortcut through Utrecht Centraal
Subject of the research. The subject of my research is the influence of media and Internet on physical space, architecture and urban planning. The starting point for the research was the building of Utrecht Central undergoing renovation, being in constant flux and getting extensions in the form of shopping mall and eventually turning into a so-called ‘Junkspace’.
2What do you want to find out about this subject? [research question] How does new media influence architecture? How does new media/electronic technology influence our perception, communication and physical space? What is the relation between media and architecture?
3What is your preliminary position on this question? [hypothesis] New media changes the perception of information, changes the way our brains are functioning and therefore affects our approach towards urban planning. Reality is merging with the abstract concepts of the orientation on the net.
