Shortcut through Utrecht Centraal

1. What subject are you researching? The subject of my research and the starting point is architecture and planning of Utrecht Central Station.  2. What do you want to find out about this subject? [research question] How does new media influence architecture?/ How does new media/electronic technology influence our perception, communication and physical space? What is the relation between media and architecture?  3. What is your preliminary position on this question? [hypothesis] New media changes the perception of information, changes the way our brains are functioning and therefore affects our approach towards urban planning. Reality is merging with the abstract concepts of the orientation on the net.  4. What are your central assumptions, your reasons for researching this subject? [starting points] The aspect of human communication has always fascinated me. I want to find out how the ever-changing media influences something so monumental as architecture. 5. How do you go about your research? What type/s of sources are you using? [research method] Observation, personal experience, asking other people about their experience, various material on urban planning and devilment of media and its influence on communication. 6. What did you find out so far? What was expected, what was unexpected? Observations at the numerous public spaces, material research on related topics. 7. What else do you expect to find out? More direct connection and visible influence and interconnection.
